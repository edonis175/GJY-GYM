Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our gym, services, and membership options

What are your operating hours?

Our gym is open Monday through Friday from 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM, Saturday from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM, and Sunday from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM. Premium and Elite members have 24/7 access.

Do you offer trial memberships?

Yes, we offer a one-day trial pass for new visitors. This allows you to experience our facilities and services before committing to a membership. Please contact us to schedule your trial visit.

What equipment do you have?

Our facility features a comprehensive range of equipment including cardio machines, free weights, weight machines, functional training equipment, and dedicated areas for stretching and mobility work.

Are personal trainers available?

Yes, we have certified personal trainers available for one-on-one sessions. Personal training is included in Premium and Elite memberships, and can be purchased separately for other membership levels.

What group classes do you offer?

We offer a variety of group classes including yoga, pilates, spinning, HIIT, and strength training classes. The class schedule varies by day and is available at the front desk or on our website.

Can I cancel my membership?

Membership cancellation policies vary by plan. Monthly memberships can typically be cancelled with 30 days notice. Please contact our membership services team for specific details about your plan.

Do you have locker rooms and showers?

Yes, we have separate locker rooms for men and women, each equipped with lockers, showers, and changing areas. Lockers are available for daily use, and long-term locker rentals are available for an additional fee.

Is parking available?

Yes, we have free parking available for all members. The parking lot is located directly adjacent to our facility and provides convenient access to the gym entrance.

Still Have Questions?

If you couldn't find the answer you're looking for, our friendly staff is here to help. Feel free to reach out to us through our contact page or visit us in person.

We're committed to providing you with all the information you need to make the best decision for your fitness journey.
